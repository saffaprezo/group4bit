About us

Group 4 Fm 98.12 The voice for the voiceless people.

G4 Fm is a community base radio station that came into existence in 2019. It was established mainly to savage the community, as the founder and CEO Mr Prezo Saffa thought it fit that since the community in which they are living is without a radio station, and therefore, he thought of bringing one to the community. His idea was honoured and appreciated by many stakeholders and most of them gave him their support in one way or the other. From that time, he allocated one of his houses, to be used as the radio stations. on the 28th of March 2019, the radio was formally registered and license to operate as a community radio station with the frequency 98.12 Fm. we have now operated for two years, from the time of registration. for the past two years, the radio has received numerous awards and most of our staffs as well. we are having 28 staffs. in terms of radio broadcasting, we are the best and we shall forever remain to be the best.

What We Do

The Group 4 Fm We do offer free air time to discussion that are around the developement of the community in which the radio station is been found.
